Sinigang na Bangus

Description

Sinigang na Bangus is a type of sour clear broth fish soup. This recipe that we have here is basic, but it is my favorite among all the versions that I have tried. My family likes this too because it is simple and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pc bangus
  • 1 pc tomato slice
  • 1 pc onion chopped
  • 1 10g Sinigang sa Sampalok mix
  • 1 bundle water spinach

Steps

  1. Pakuluan ang sibuyas at kamatis sa tubig.
  2. Idagdag ang bangus at pakuluin ng 5 minuto.
  3. Idagdag ang sinigang mix at kangkong.
